Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis

Combining AI & healthcare for instant disease prediction.

Overview

Our Flask-powered web app leverages a Support Vector Machine (SVM) model to predict diseases based on user symptoms, providing quick and reliable health insights.

How It Works

  1. Input Symptoms: Users enter symptoms in an intuitive UI.
  2. AI Prediction: A trained SVM model predicts potential diseases.
  3. Comprehensive Info: Users receive disease details, precautions, and health tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly: Simple and seamless interface.
  • Accurate AI Diagnosis: Machine learning ensures precision.
  • Health Insights: Includes medication, diet, and workout recommendations.

Conclusion

This web app bridges AI and healthcare, offering fast and accessible disease predictions to empower users in making informed health decisions.
